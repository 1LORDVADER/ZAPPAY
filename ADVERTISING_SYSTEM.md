## Non-Invasive Ad Placement Strategy

**Core Principles:**
1. **Never interrupt shopping flow** - No pop-ups, no interstitials
2. **Native integration** - Ads look like organic content
3. **Relevant targeting** - Only show ads for products users want
4. **Frequency capping** - Max 3 ads per page, max 10 ads per session
5. **Respect user intent** - No ads on checkout pages

**Ad Placements:**
- Homepage: Hero banner (1), sidebar (2), between product rows (3)
- Product pages: Sidebar (2), related products section (2)
- Search results: Top 3 sponsored listings (clearly labeled)
- Cart page: "Customers also bought" (2 sponsored products)
- Email/SMS: 1 sponsored product per campaign (opt-in only)
