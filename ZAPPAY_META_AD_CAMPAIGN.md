## Compliance Strategy

ZAPPAY is advertising a **B2B technology platform**, not cannabis products. This is the critical distinction that keeps ads compliant with Meta policy. The ads must never mention THC, cannabis strains, or product sales. Every ad frames ZAPPAY as a **payment processing and marketplace network for licensed agricultural operators**.

**What is allowed:**
- Advertising a business network or marketplace for licensed operators
- Lead generation for a B2B SaaS or platform product
- Mentioning "hemp," "agricultural," or "licensed grower" in copy
- Driving traffic to a waitlist or sign-up page

**What must be avoided in all ad copy and creative:**
- The words: cannabis, marijuana, THC, weed, dispensary, high, intoxicating
- Images of cannabis leaves, buds, or smoking
- Any reference to recreational effects or psychoactive properties

## Step-by-Step Setup in Meta Ads Manager

1. Go to [Meta Ads Manager](https://www.facebook.com/adsmanager) → **Create Campaign**
2. Select objective: **Leads**
3. Name the campaign: `ZAPPAY Grower Acquisition Q2 2026`
4. Under Campaign Budget Optimization: **OFF** (set budget at ad set level)
5. Create **Ad Set A** with the targeting above, $12/day budget
6. Create **Ad Set B** with the targeting above, $8/day budget
7. Under each ad set, create **2–3 ad variants** using the copy above
8. Set the Pixel event to optimize for: **Lead** (Pixel ID: `929348566628146`)
9. Set campaign start date and end date (14 days)
10. Submit for review — Meta typically approves B2B lead gen ads within 24 hours

**After 3–4 days:** Pause the lowest-performing ad variant and double budget on the winner.
