## 1. Introduction

ZAPPAY ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our payment processing and marketplace facilitation platform.

By using ZAPPAY, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you create an account or make a purchase:

- **Name:** First and last name
- **Email Address:** For account creation, order confirmations, and communications
- **Phone Number:** For order updates and customer support
- **Date of Birth:** To verify you are 21 years or older
- **Delivery Address:** To fulfill orders (must be in a legal cannabis state)
- **Government-Issued ID:** For age verification at delivery

### Payment Information
- **Credit/Debit Card Information:** Processed securely by Stripe, Inc. ZAPPAY does not store your full credit card number, CVV, or expiration date.
- **Billing Address:** Collected for payment processing
- **Transaction History:** Records of purchases, amounts, and dates

### Location Information
- **IP Address:** Used for geolocation verification to confirm you are in a legal cannabis state
- **Geolocation Data:** Approximate location based on IP address
- **Delivery Location:** Verified to ensure compliance with state laws

### Usage Information
- **Browsing History:** Pages visited, products viewed, search queries
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Session data, preferences, and analytics

### Farmer and Transporter Information
For users with Farmer or Transporter accounts, we additionally collect:
- **Business Name and License Number:** To verify legal operation
- **Tax Identification Number (EIN/SSN):** For payment processing and tax reporting
- **Bank Account Information:** For ACH payouts (securely stored and encrypted)
- **Product Listings:** Strain information, THC/CBD content, pricing, lab results (COAs)

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### With Stripe (Payment Processor)
All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. When you make a purchase, your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe. Stripe's use of your information is governed by their Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

We share with Stripe:
- Name, email, billing address
- Transaction amount and details
- Payment method information

### With Farmers and Transporters
To fulfill your order, we share:
- Name, delivery address, phone number
- Order details (products, quantities, delivery instructions)
- Age verification status (21+ confirmed)

Farmers and transporters are contractually obligated to use this information only for order fulfillment and to protect your privacy.

### With Law Enforcement
We may disclose information to law enforcement or government authorities if:
- Required by law, subpoena, or court order
- Necessary to investigate fraud or illegal activity
- Required to protect the safety of users or the public
- Necessary to enforce our Terms of Service

### With Service Providers
We share information with third-party service providers who assist with:
- Hosting and infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud)
- Analytics (Google Analytics)
- Email communications (SendGrid, Mailchimp)
- Customer support (Zendesk)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### Business Transfers
If ZAPPAY is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such change.

## 5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

### Encryption
- All data transmitted between your browser and ZAPPAY is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- Payment information is encrypted and processed by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 compliant)
- Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption

### Access Controls
- Employee access to personal information is restricted on a need-to-know basis
- Multi-factor authentication required for administrative access
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

### Secure Storage
- Data stored on secure servers with firewalls and intrusion detection
- Regular backups to prevent data loss
- Compliance with PCI DSS requirements via Stripe

### Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:
- Notify you via email within 72 hours of discovery
- Provide details about the breach and affected information
- Offer guidance on protective measures
- Report the breach to relevant authorities as required by law

**No security system is perfect.** While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. State-Specific Privacy Rights

### California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
- Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, email privacy@zappay.com or call 1-800-ZAPPAY-1.

### Nevada Residents
Nevada residents may opt out of the sale of personal information. ZAPPAY does not sell personal information, but you may submit an opt-out request to privacy@zappay.com.

### Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Utah Residents
Residents of these states have rights similar to California under their respective state privacy laws. Contact us to exercise your rights.
