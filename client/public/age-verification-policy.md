# Age Verification Policy

**Effective Date:** February 15, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026

---

## 1. Legal Requirement

Federal and state laws require that cannabis products be sold only to individuals who are **21 years of age or older** (18+ in some states for medical use). ZAPPAY is committed to full compliance with all age verification laws.

By using ZAPPAY, you certify that you are of legal age to purchase cannabis products in your state.

---

## 2. Age Verification Methods

ZAPPAY uses multiple methods to verify your age:

### Online Age Gate
- When you first visit ZAPPAY, you will be prompted to confirm you are 21+ years of age
- You must click "Yes, I am 21 or older" to access the Platform
- This verification is stored in your browser session or for 30 days if you select "Remember me"

### Geolocation Verification
- We use your IP address to determine your approximate location
- Access is restricted to users in states where cannabis is legal
- If you are located in a state where cannabis is illegal, you will not be able to access the Platform

### Government-Issued ID at Delivery
- All deliveries require presentation of a valid government-issued photo ID
- Acceptable IDs include:
  - Driver's license
  - State ID card
  - Passport
  - Military ID
- The delivery person will verify that:
  - The ID is valid and not expired
  - The photo matches the recipient
  - The recipient is 21+ years of age
- If you cannot provide valid ID, the product will not be delivered and no refund will be issued

### Information Collected
For age verification purposes, we collect:

### How We Use This Information
- To verify you are 21+ years of age

- To maintain records for regulatory audits

### Data Storage
- Age verification data is stored securely and encrypted
- Data is retained for 3 years to demonstrate compliance with cannabis laws
- See our Privacy Policy for more details on data handling

## 4. Consequences of False Information

Providing false information about your age or location is a violation of our Terms of Service and may be illegal. Consequences include:

- Immediate account termination
- Ban from future use of ZAPPAY
- Forfeiture of any pending orders (no refund)
- Reporting to law enforcement if required by law

**Do not attempt to bypass age verification.** We take compliance seriously.

## 6. Session Storage and Cookies

### Session Storage
- Your age verification status is stored in your browser session
- This allows you to browse the Platform without repeated verification
- Session data is cleared when you close your browser

### "Remember Me" Option
- If you select "Remember me for 30 days," your verification is stored in a cookie
- This cookie expires after 30 days
- You can clear this cookie at any time through your browser settings

### Cookie Control
- You can disable cookies through your browser settings
- Disabling age verification cookies will require you to verify your age each time you visit ZAPPAY

---

## 7. Delivery Age Verification

### ID Requirements
- The person receiving the delivery must be 21+ years of age
- A valid government-issued photo ID must be presented
- The ID must not be expired

### Delivery Refusal
If the delivery person determines that:
- The recipient is under 21 years of age
- The ID is invalid, expired, or does not match the recipient
- The recipient refuses to show ID

**The product will not be delivered and no refund will be issued.**

### Third-Party Delivery
- If someone other than the purchaser is receiving the delivery, they must also be 21+ and present valid ID
- The delivery person has the right to refuse delivery if they suspect the recipient is underage

## 8. State-Specific Requirements

Age verification requirements vary by state. ZAPPAY complies with the most stringent requirements to ensure full compliance across all states where we operate.

### Medical Cannabis States
- Some states allow medical cannabis use for individuals 18+ with a valid medical card
- ZAPPAY currently requires all users to be 21+ regardless of medical status
- We may add medical cannabis support in the future
