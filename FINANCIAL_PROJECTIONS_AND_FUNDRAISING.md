# ZAPPAY Financial Projections & Fundraising Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Business Model:** B2B2C marketplace connecting licensed cannabis farmers directly with consumers through verified dispensaries, charging 5.2% commission on all transactions.

**Market Opportunity:** $33.6B US cannabis market (2024), growing to $57B by 2028. Current retail markup is 65-75%, creating massive arbitrage opportunity.

**Competitive Advantage:** Direct farmer-to-consumer pricing eliminates retail overhead, offering products 65-75% cheaper than dispensaries while farmers keep 94.8% of revenue.

**Capital Need:** $500K-$2M seed funding for 18-month runway

**Use of Funds:** Platform development (30%), farmer acquisition (40%), marketing (20%), operations (10%)
