## Critical Features (Must-Have)

### 1. Age Gate & Compliance
- Legal requirement for 21+ verification
- Simple "Are you 21+?" pop-up minimum
- ID verification on delivery/pickup

### 2. Mobile-First Design
- **70%+ of transactions happen on mobile**
- Fast loading speed
- Responsive layout
- Easy tap interactions
- Clear CTAs

### 3. Product Display Best Practices
- **High-quality product photos** (professional, well-lit)
- **Proper aspect ratios** (square or 4:3 for consistency)
- **Zoom functionality** on product pages
- Multiple product angles/views
- Lifestyle imagery showing products in context
- Clear product information (THC%, CBD%, strain type)

### 4. Navigation & UX
- Sticky header with navigation
- Clear product categorization
- Search functionality
- Filter by: price, THC%, strain type, effect
- Bold, action-oriented CTAs
- Simplified cart experience

### 5. SEO-Optimized Menus
- Native e-commerce (NOT iFrame embeds)
- Google-discoverable product pages
- Educational content for SEO boost
- Meta descriptions and alt text

### 6. E-commerce Features
- Live inventory display
- Real-time pricing
- Click-and-collect
- Curbside pickup
- Delivery options
- Simplified checkout flow

### 7. Trust Signals
- Store locations with maps
- Hours of operation
- Contact information
- Educational content
- Customer reviews/ratings
- Lab test results

## ZAPPAY's Competitive Advantages

### What We Do Better
1. **Direct farmer-to-consumer pricing** (65-75% cheaper than retail)
2. **Bulk pricing for everyone** (no minimum order restrictions)
3. **5.2% platform fee** (vs 15-30% retail markup)
4. **Nationwide marketplace** (50+ legal states)

### Weaknesses to Fix
1. ❌ **Duplicate products** in database
2. ❌ **Poor image quality** (cropping issues, not full frame)
3. ❌ **No image zoom** on product pages
4. ❌ **No age gate** (legal requirement)
5. ❌ **No sticky navigation**
6. ❌ **No advanced filtering** (by THC%, price range, effects)
7. ❌ **No customer reviews** (trust signal)
8. ❌ **No educational content** (SEO opportunity)
9. ❌ **No mobile optimization testing**
10. ❌ **Incomplete checkout flow** (Stripe not fully integrated)

## Design Improvements Needed

### Product Images
- Use **square aspect ratio** (1:1) for consistency
- Professional product photography with proper lighting
- White or neutral backgrounds
- Show product in full frame (no cropping)
- Add zoom functionality on hover/click
- Multiple angles for flower products

### Layout
- Add sticky navigation header
- Implement product grid with consistent spacing
- Use lifestyle imagery in hero sections
- Add "Quick View" modal for fast browsing
- Implement infinite scroll or pagination

### Typography & Branding
- Bold, clear product names
- Readable pricing (large, prominent)
- Consistent color palette throughout
- Professional font pairing

### Trust & Conversion
- Add customer reviews/ratings
- Display lab test results
- Show "verified farmer" badges
- Add security badges at checkout
- Include money-back guarantee
